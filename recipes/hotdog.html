Jeff's Hot Dog Chili

Hot dog chili tends to be slightly sweeter than regular chili that
you'd eat from a bowl. Also, it's usually made with ketchup.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Water
  • Tomato products
  • Seasonings
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in water.
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer until the beef is cooked and the chili is thick.
